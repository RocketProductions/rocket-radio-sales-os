# Product Vision: Rocket Radio Sales OS

Rocket Radio Sales OS is a unified operating system for radio stations and local media companies. It empowers sales teams to take a client’s objective – whether brand awareness, lead generation or event attendance – and produce a campaign plan, creative assets and reports in minutes.

## Users

- **Account Executives** – gather client information and generate proposals, scripts and creative assets.
- **Sales Managers** – review and approve campaigns, track pipeline and team performance.
- **Copywriters & Creatives** – refine AI‑generated scripts and assets.
- **Fulfilment Teams** – launch campaigns across radio and digital channels.
- **Advertisers / Clients** – receive proposals, approve creatives and view reports.
- **Leadership** – monitor revenue, client retention and station performance.

## Modules

1. **Client Intake Engine** – collects business details, goals, audience, budget and seasonality, then generates a structured brief.
2. **Campaign Builder** – recommends channel mixes (radio schedule, digital placements, contests, email and social) based on the brief.
3. **Creative Studio** – produces draft scripts, live reads, emails, social posts and landing page copy.
4. **Proposal Builder** – assembles branded proposal documents with package tiers, pricing and timelines.
5. **Fulfilment Handoff** – converts sold packages into tasks for traffic, production and digital teams.
6. **Reporting & Renewal** – pulls campaign performance data, builds recaps and suggests renewal pitches.

## Pricing Tiers

- **Rep Pro** – for individual sellers; includes intake, campaign builder and creative studio.
- **Station Team** – for clusters or groups; adds approvals, shared templates and reporting dashboards.
- **Enterprise** – for agencies and large broadcasters; includes multi‑brand workspaces, custom playbooks and white‑label exports.

## Roadmap

1. **Phase 1: Internal Seller Tool** – build intake, campaign builder, creative studio and proposal builder.
2. **Phase 2: Team Operating System** – add approvals, fulfilment handoff, shared templates and manager dashboards.
3. **Phase 3: Client Portal** – provide clients with proposal views, campaign status and reports.
4. **Phase 4: White‑Label SaaS** – support multi‑tenant billing, branding and custom templates.
